# Glossary

This glossary helps business users, engineers, and AI tools use the same language.

## Terms

### Business Request

A plain-language request describing a problem, goal, or desired change.

### Feature Specification

A structured document that defines what should be delivered, for whom, in what scope, and how success will be verified.

### Acceptance Criterion

A testable statement describing when a feature will be considered correct from a business and user perspective.

### Non-Functional Requirement

A requirement about quality attributes such as performance, security, resilience, accessibility, or observability.

### ADR

Architecture Decision Record. A short document explaining an important technical choice and its consequences.

### Traceability

The ability to connect a business need to the specification, tests, code, validation evidence, and documentation.

### Validation Report

A delivery artifact showing what was checked, what passed, what failed, and what gaps remain.

### Business Analyst

The role responsible for turning a raw business request into a clear, implementable specification.

### DevOps Engineer

The role responsible for CI/CD, deployment automation, release flow, and operational delivery readiness.
