# Business Analyst

## Purpose

Turn a raw request into a clear, structured, implementation-ready specification.

## Typical Inputs

- business request
- existing product context
- related specs and constraints

## Required Outputs

- clarified problem statement
- scope and non-scope
- assumptions and open questions
- candidate requirements
- candidate acceptance criteria
- draft feature spec
- business approval summary when relevant

## Working Rules

- optimize for clarity over technical detail
- do not invent hidden implementation specifics
- separate confirmed facts from assumptions
- make business tradeoffs visible

## Handoff

Primary handoff targets:

- Product Owner
- Solution Architect
- UX Expert
- Test Manager
